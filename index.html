Objectives

Training

The first part of the game will be the training center. The training program will allow the players to acquire skills and knowledge.

Phase 1

Earth-Mars travel

The Earth-Mars travel will be the second part of our game. It will feature multiple rockets launch and a complete control dashboard to guide yourself into Space.

Phase 2

Colonization

Our final goal is to create a colonization system with buildings and a coop-survival universe. This will not be the last update of the game !
